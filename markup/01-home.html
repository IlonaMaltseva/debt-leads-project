How we qualify leads

Qualified consumer proposal leads delivered to your inbox or CRM

Our marketing is focussed on qualifying people for and selling the benefits of filing a consumer proposal. This makes our leads ideal for debt advisors and Licensed Insolvency Trustees. Our leads meet the following qualifications.

Our Leads

  • Have a minimum of $10,000 in unsecured debt.

  • Are within your targeted geographic region.
